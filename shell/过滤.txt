从 ftp 下载 regular_express.txt：
过滤下载文件中包含 the 关键字       (grep "the" regular_express.txt )
过滤下载文件中不包含 the 关键字     (grep -v "the" regular_express.txt )
过滤下载文件中不论大小写 the 关键字  (grep -i "the" regular_express.txt)
过滤 test 或 taste 这两个单字    (egrep "test|taste" regular_express.txt)
                             (grep "t[ae]ste\{0,1\}" regular_express.txt)
过滤有 oo 的字节         (grep "oo" regular_express.txt)
过滤不想要 oo 前面有 g 的    (grep/egrep "[^g]oo" regular_express.txt)
过滤 oo 前面不想有小写字节   (grep "[^a-z]oo" regular_express.txt)
过滤有数字的那一行        (grep "[0-9]" regular_express.txt) 
过滤以 the 开头的        (grep "^the" regular_express.txt)
过滤以小写字母开头的      (grep "^[a-z]" regular_express.txt)
过滤开头不是英文字母      (grep "^[^a-zA-Z]" regular_express.txt)
过滤行尾结束为小数点.那一行   (grep "\.$" regular_express.txt)
过滤空白行                 ( grep "^$" regular_express.txt )
过滤出 g??d 的字串        (egrep "g..d" regular_express.txt)
过滤至少两个 o 以上的字串    (egrep "(o{2})+" regular_express.txt) 
                         (grep "ooo*" regular_express.txt)
过滤 g 开头和 g 结尾但是两个 g 之间仅存在至少一个 o   (grep "goo*g"  regular_express.txt)
过滤任意数字的行    (grep "[0-9]" regular_express.txt)
过滤两个 o 的字串   (grep "o\{2\}" regular_express.txt)
过滤 g 后面接 2 到 5 个 o，然后在接一个 g 的字串   (grep "go\{2,5\}g" regular_express.txt )
过滤 g 后面接 2 个以上 o 的   (grep "go\{2,\}" regular_express.txt)
